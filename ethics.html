Ethical Principles in IT

Developer Responsibilities

Responsible developers follow core ethical guidelines like prioritizing user safety and respecting digital rights. As noted by the ACM Code of Ethics, they should act in the public interest and maintain data confidentiality (ACM, 2018).

Data Ethics in Practice

When working with data, professionals must ensure fairness and transparency. The UK Government's Data Ethics Framework encourages accountability and integrity in data use (UK Gov, 2020).
